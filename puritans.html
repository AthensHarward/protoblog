Common Stereotypes
I am mostly focused on a broad, bird's eye view of the movement, so I will not be spending much time exploring the truth of sterotypes.
***
Whether or not people were aware of it or not, two well known roles of the Puritans in history were as the Pilgrims that landed at Plymouth Rock and as the instigators of the Salem Witch Trials. As far as people's opinion of them, both past and present, they are known being extremely prudish and morally strict. In fact, this is where we get the word Puritanical, which references their strong work ethic in addtion to their abhorence of pleasure.
