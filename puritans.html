Intentions

The purpose of these articles, is to give a basic overview of background information to aid in understanding the context of specific events or characters. It is not intended for an exhaustive study

Common Stereotypes
I am mostly focused on a broad, bird's eye view of the movement, so I will not be spending much time exploring the truth of sterotypes.
********************************* Some common perceptions of the Puritans are their roles as the Pilgrims that landed at Plymouth Rock and as the instigators of the Salem Witch Trials. They were also very well known for extremely strict and prudish morals. In fact, the word Puritanical, coined in 1598, is a direct reflection of public opinion of them.
